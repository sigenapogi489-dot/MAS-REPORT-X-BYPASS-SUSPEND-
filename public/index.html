It is not advisable to use your actual or personal account for a session, as it may get restricted or suspended. Please use an account you don't use often instead.

About

This tool helps you get more reactions on Facebook posts. Just paste the link, choose a reaction, and let it handle the rest.

A post can get up to 30 reactions, but it might not always hit that number as some reactions may fail to send. You can only use the same account (session) 5 times per day. After each successful use, there’s a 15-minute cooldown on that account (session), so you won’t be able to use it again until the cooldown ends or you switch to a different account.

Notice

You might notice some accounts simply changing their reaction without new reactions being added. This happens because we currently have a low number of users. Please help us grow by sharing this site with your friends.
